About Geo-ify

Basics

Geo-ify is a Web-App that takes in a users Spotify data and outputs the cities that are most closely correlated with the users top artists's genres.

A Note on City/Genre Correlation

Geo-ify uses a database of City Genre correlations from the brilliant Every Noise at Once Project. The specific collection is called We Built This City On. Find it Here.
In the creators own words the site is a "a distribution of the genres tracked by Spotify to the 322 cities that, according to our data, produced notable fractions of their definitive artists." While it is unclear how a genre's "definitive" artists are identified the logic behind the correlations is (I think) straightforward, for example a city that produces many popular Experimental Jazz Panists (unsure if this is real) would be highly correlated with the genre experimental Jazz Piano and would find itself listed as a city with such a correlation. Then Geo-ify may find a musician in your top musicians who is an Experimental Jazz Pianist and would then say oh well Experimental Jazz Piano is from [a city].
This can lead to some confusiom especially about artists who are famously from one city but perform a genre more strongly correlated with another city.

A note on Geo-ify Doing Its Best

Geo-ify is a little Python Flask App written by a novice coder who likes maps and music and is only as good as its data and sometimes improvisational and messy code. it's not gonna do the entire intersection of geography, music and culture justice. I apologize on it's behalf for any oversights and hope as the project of identifying genres continues over at Every Noise a wider and wider view of music and its heritage continues to come into view.
